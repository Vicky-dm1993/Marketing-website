E-commerce SEO Optimization

Client

FashionRetail Inc.

Timeline

6 months

Services

SEO, Content Strategy, Technical SEO

Project Overview

FashionRetail Inc., an established online clothing retailer, was struggling with declining organic traffic and search engine rankings. They approached us to develop and implement a comprehensive SEO strategy to improve their visibility in search results and drive more qualified traffic to their website.

Challenge

The client faced several challenges, including:

  • Declining organic traffic despite having quality products
  • Poor technical SEO implementation causing indexing issues
  • Lack of optimized content for target keywords
  • Strong competition in a crowded e-commerce fashion market

Solution

We developed a multi-faceted SEO strategy that included:

  • Comprehensive technical SEO audit and implementation of fixes
  • Keyword research and content optimization for product pages
  • Development of a content marketing strategy targeting fashion-related keywords
  • Implementation of schema markup for products to enhance search results
  • Internal linking structure optimization
  • Mobile optimization to improve user experience

Results

  • 200% increase in organic traffic within 6 months
  • First page rankings for over 100 target keywords
  • 45% increase in organic conversions
  • Improved site speed by 60%, enhancing user experience
  • 30% reduction in bounce rate
